3-Day Exploration Itinerary

A comprehensive plan to experience beaches, heritage, and local culture.

01

Day 1: Beaches & Surf Culture

Morning: Surf at Arugam Bay Main Point. Visit Baby Point for beginners.

Afternoon: Explore Elephant Rock and Crocodile Rock. Relax at Peanut Farm Beach.

Evening: Sunset at Main Point View Deck. Dinner at Arugam Bay Main Street.

02

Day 2: Heritage & Nature

Morning: Visit Darampanawa Ruins, 3rd Mile Post Ruins, and Sengamuwa sites.

Afternoon: Pottuvil Lagoon boat safari. Explore Ruhuna Mangrove Forest.

Evening: Visit Pottuvil Central Mosque and St Francis Xavier Church.

03

Day 3: Local Experiences

Morning: Pottuvil Local Market tour. Batik workshop visit.

Afternoon: Urani Village tour. Paddy field tuk-tuk ride.

Evening: Yoga session. Live music at Beach Bar.
